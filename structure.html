My tanguar haowr tour

Travelling to Tanguar Haor and staying in the haor area itself makes one encounter the essence of Bangladesh:
the living together with the forces of the water that creates all life in Bangladesh and at the same time is a destructive force.
The haor area gets flooded every year during the rainy season.
The area is one enormous lake then (from April to October).
When the water retreats the remaining lakes constitute a resting place for 100.000 of migratory birds (November-February)
You can visit Tanguar Haor in both seaons for a completely different experience.
Visits should be made by boat. A one day trip is the minimum but longer trips are possible as boats can provide food and lodging.
